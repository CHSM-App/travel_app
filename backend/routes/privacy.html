This Privacy Policy explains how VengurlaTech (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you use the VengurlaTech Travel Agency Management application and related services (the “Service”). The Service is a B2B tool that lets a travel-agency administrator manage their own customers, drivers, vehicles, and trip bookings.

i
Who is the user? The Service is operated by an authorised administrator of a travel agency. The agency itself controls the personal data of its customers, drivers, and staff that it enters into the Service. VengurlaTech acts as the data processor on the agency's behalf for that information.

01Information we collect

1.1 Administrator account information

  • Full name, mobile number, email address
  • Password (stored encrypted; never displayed or shared)
  • Agency name, address, city
  • Profile photo (optional, uploaded by the administrator)

1.2 Information the administrator enters about others

  • Customers — name, phone number, address, driving licence number, licence expiry, and ID-proof document (uploaded image or PDF).
  • Drivers — name, phone number, address, licence number, licence expiry, and licence document.
  • Vehicles — vehicle name, registration number, type, capacity, fuel type, mileage, and RC document.
  • Trip bookings — pickup/drop locations, distance, fuel required, dates & times, trip charges, toll/repair/driver charges, and payment status.

1.3 Technical & usage information

  • Device details (model, OS version) provided during sign-in to bind sessions to a specific device.
  • IP address and timestamps recorded by server access logs for security and abuse-prevention purposes.
  • Crash and diagnostic information when something goes wrong.

1.4 Information we do not collect

  • Real-time GPS location of the user's device.
  • Contacts, photos, or other on-device data outside the documents the administrator explicitly chooses to upload.
  • Advertising identifiers.

02How we use information

  • To provide the Service and its core features (manage trips, customers, drivers, vehicles, billing, history).
  • To authenticate users and protect accounts (password-based login, refresh tokens, device binding).
  • To process payments through our payment partner.
  • To send transactional messages (OTPs, password resets, booking confirmations) via SMS where applicable.
  • To maintain reliability, debug issues, and improve the Service.
  • To comply with applicable laws, court orders, and regulatory requirements.

03How we share information

We do not sell personal data. We share limited information only with the following categories of recipients, strictly to operate the Service:

  • Payment gateway — Razorpay Software Pvt. Ltd. (when a payment is processed). Razorpay receives transaction data but not your full address book or document uploads.
  • SMS provider — used to deliver OTP and transactional messages to the phone number provided.
  • Hosting and infrastructure providers — our servers and database are hosted with reputable providers in India under appropriate contractual safeguards.
  • Push-notification provider — Google Firebase Cloud Messaging is used solely to deliver notifications to the installed app.
  • Legal authorities — when required by law, court order, or to investigate fraud or violations of our terms.

04Document uploads

Identity documents (driving licences, RC books, ID proofs) uploaded by the administrator are stored on our servers under access-controlled directories scoped to the uploading agency. Only the originating agency and authorised VengurlaTech personnel can access them. Uploaded files are served over HTTPS.

05Data security

  • All traffic between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS / TLS.
  • Sessions use short-lived access tokens and rotated refresh tokens.
  • Access to production data is limited to authorised personnel on a need-to-know basis.
  • We continuously review our infrastructure for vulnerabilities and apply updates as appropriate.

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we use reasonable measures to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

06Data retention

We retain personal data for as long as the administrator's account is active and the data remains useful for operating the Service. Upon account deletion, related personal data is removed or anonymised within a reasonable period unless we are required to retain it for legal, tax, accounting, or fraud-prevention purposes.

07Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (including, for users in India, rights under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention obligations).
  • Withdraw consent for processing where consent is the lawful basis.
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant data-protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below. We will respond within a reasonable time frame.

08Children's privacy

The Service is intended for use by adult travel-agency administrators. It is not directed at children under the age of 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

09Cookies & tracking

The mobile application itself does not use browser cookies. Our web components (if any) may use functional cookies strictly necessary for the service to operate. We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies.

10International data transfers

Our servers are located in India. If you access the Service from outside India, your information will be transferred to and processed in India under the safeguards described in this Policy.

11Permissions used by the mobile app

  • Camera — to photograph identity documents at the time of upload (used only when you tap “Camera”).
  • Photos / Storage — to let you pick existing documents or images for upload.
  • Internet — to communicate with our servers.
  • Notifications — to deliver booking and payment alerts.

12Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the application or via email to registered administrators. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
